# Cashback

Earn **20% cashback** on every trade you make on Builders.meme.

## How It Works

Every time you buy or sell a token on the bonding curve, 20% of the 1% trading fee is returned to you as cashback.

### Example
- You buy 1 BNB of tokens
- Trading fee: 0.01 BNB (1%)
- Your cashback: 0.002 BNB (20% of fee)

## Multi-Token Cashback

Cashback is paid in the **same token** as the trading pair:
- Trading BNB pairs → cashback in BNB
- Trading USDT pairs → cashback in USDT
- Trading CAKE pairs → cashback in CAKE

## Claiming

1. Go to the **Cashback** page
2. View your unclaimed balance (shows USD value)
3. Click **Claim Cashback**
4. All tokens are claimed in a single transaction

## Fee Breakdown

The 1% trading fee is split:

| Recipient | Share | Description |
|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Platform | 70% | Platform revenue |
| Cashback | 20% | Returned to trader |
| Referral | 10% | Paid to referrer (if any) |

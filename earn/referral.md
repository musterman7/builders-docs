# Referral Program

Earn **10% of trading fees** from users who trade using your referral link.

## How It Works

1. **Get Your Code** — Visit your Profile page and find your unique referral code
2. **Share Your Link** — Share your referral link: `builders.meme/#/?ref=YOUR_CODE`
3. **Earn Rewards** — When someone clicks your link and trades, you earn 10% of their trading fees automatically

## Reward Details

- Trading fee is **1%** of each trade
- Your referral reward is **10% of that fee** (0.1% of trade volume)
- Rewards are deposited into the Referral Vault
- Claim anytime from your Profile → Referral tab

## How Binding Works

When a user clicks your referral link:
1. Your referral code is saved in their browser
2. When they connect their wallet, the binding happens automatically
3. The binding is permanent — once bound, all their future trades earn you rewards
4. Binding is recorded both in the database and on-chain

## Per-Token Referral Links

You can also share token-specific referral links from any token's detail page using the **Copy Referral Link** button.

## Reward Expiry

Unclaimed referral rewards expire after **30 days**. Make sure to claim regularly.

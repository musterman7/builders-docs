# Builders.meme

**The Fair Launch Platform on BNB Smart Chain**

Builders.meme is a decentralized token launchpad on BNB Smart Chain. Anyone can create a token with built-in bonding curve mechanics, customizable tokenomics, and automatic graduation to PancakeSwap — all in one transaction.

## Why Builders.meme?

- **Fair Launch** — Every token starts at the same price with a transparent bonding curve. No presale, no insider allocation.
- **Auto-Graduation** — When the bonding curve fills, liquidity is automatically deposited to PancakeSwap and LP tokens are burned forever.
- **Built-in Tokenomics** — Configure buy/sell tax, auto-burn, dividend rewards, and liquidity generation at creation.
- **Multi-Pair Support** — Launch tokens paired with BNB, USDT, USDC, USD1, U, CAKE, or FIST.
- **Fully Renounced** — Token contracts are immutable after deployment. No rug pull possible.
- **Referral & Cashback** — Earn 10% of trading fees through referrals and 20% cashback on your own trades.
